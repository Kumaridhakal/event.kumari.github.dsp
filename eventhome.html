Why this music fastival is held?

The event is aimed at bringing both local communities and foreign visitors together to experience and promote Bhutanese culture, music, and art. It is also
an opportunity for the local communities to embrace and celebrate their diversity. yonphula Tshechu is also one of the most popular fastibal held in trashigang dzongkhag.
